Types of Weather

  • Sunny Weather

    Sunny weather occurs when the sky is clear and the sun shines brightly.

    __

  • Rainy Weather

    Rainy weather is characterized by rainfall. Be prepared with an umbrella.

    __

  • Cloudy Weather

    Cloudy weather means the sky is covered with clouds, often leading to cooler temperatures.

    __

  • Stormy Weather

    Stormy weather includes strong winds and possible thunderstorms, which can cause significant weather changes.
