Weather Tips

Weather Type Tip
Rainy Carry an umbrella and wear waterproof shoes
Sunny Wear sunglasses and apply sunscreen
Cold Dress in layers and wear a warm jacket
Windy Secure loose items and avoid open areas
Snowy Wear boots with good grip and walk carefully
